PolyPie

PolyPie is a simple web app that lets you easily set up and play polyrhythms and polymeters, while visualizing them on sliced circles, which we call Pies. The Pies rotate in sync with the sounds, and they are designed to help you understand what it is exactly that you're hearing. Go on and play with it, and if you need some additional information on what does what on this page, you can find it below.
